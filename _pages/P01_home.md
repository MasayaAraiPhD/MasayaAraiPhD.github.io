I am a postdoctoral researcher studying mucosal immunology, T cell biology, and food allergy.

My research focuses on understanding how immune tolerance and allergic responses are established in the gastrointestinal tract. In particular, I'm investigating the mechanisms that regulate peripheral regulatory T cell differentiation, food allergy, and antigen-specific immune responses using experimental mouse models.
